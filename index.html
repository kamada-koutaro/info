Project

Group Discussion (2022-)

We study how to make group discussions—face-to-face, text, video, and VR—work better for everyone. Our focus is group dynamics (e.g., social loafing). Through experiments and data analysis, we identify why this happens and provide practical CMC tool designs that counter it.

Keywords: Remote Collaboration, CMC, Group Dynamics, Social Loafing

Human-AI Collaborative Decision-Making (2023-)

AI tools can improve decisions, but over-reliance can reduce decision quality. We study and build human-AI interfaces that foster appropriate trust and help people use AI well. The goal: help people and AI make better decisions together.

Keywords: Human-AI Collaboration, AI-Based Decision-Making

VR System for Psychiatric Disorders (2023-)

We are developing a VR system for mental disorders. For example, we aim to measure social cognitive functions related to schizophrenia in a more realistic way using VR.

Keywords: VR, Social Cognition, Psychiatric Disorder
